  2. Kothe momo

    Kothe momo, a beloved Nepali delicacy, tantalizes taste buds with its flavorful filling encased in delicate dough. These bite-sized dumplings, typically pan-fried to perfection, are a delightful fusion of savory spices and tender meat or vegetable fillings.

Prizes:

The highly anticipated food contest boasts enticing rewards for its participants, with a grand prize of €5000 awaiting the champion, along with the esteemed title of culinary excellence. The runner-up isn't left empty-handed either, as they claim a commendable €2000 prize for their culinary prowess. Additionally, other top contenders are also recognized for their exceptional talents, with generous prizes awarded accordingly, fostering a spirit of celebration and camaraderie within the culinary community.

Basic Rules:

The rules of the competition dictate that participants must showcase their culinary prowess by creating delectable momos, the quintessential Nepali delicacy, within a tight timeframe of just one hour. While creativity is encouraged, adherence to traditional momo-making techniques and ingredients remains paramount, ensuring authenticity and honoring the rich heritage of Nepali cuisine.
