Each momo bite tells a story of culinary heritage, blending tradition with innovation. As anticipation mounts, food enthusiasts gather, eager to sample the offerings and witness culinary magic. Barcelona becomes a cultural crossroads, where Nepali flavors harmonize with the city's vibes, creating an unforgettable experience for all.

  1. Steam momo

    Steamed momo, a Nepali culinary delight, features tender dough parcels enveloping succulent fillings, offering a delectable burst of flavors in every bite.

  2. Kothe momo

    Kothe momo, a beloved Nepali delicacy, tantalizes taste buds with its flavorful filling encased in delicate dough. These bite-sized dumplings, typically pan-fried to perfection, are a delightful fusion of savory spices and tender meat or vegetable fillings.

  3. Momo chilly

    A spicy rendition of this classic dish, adds a fiery kick to the traditional recipe, creating a tantalizing harmony of heat and savory goodness.
